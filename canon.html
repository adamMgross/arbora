The Canon

Curated texts, sources, and further reading

The primary sources of Arbora are ancient: Stoic philosophy, Buddhist suttas, the Tao Te Ching, Jewish wisdom literature, Sufi poetry, the Upanishads, Ecclesiastes, indigenous cosmologies. These traditions are the trunk. Contemporary nature writing and science appear later in this canon as companions — modern voices that reach toward the same truths.

Classical Philosophy & Sacred Texts

The trunk — passages from ancient traditions that have been read and reread for millennia because they articulate truths about how to live that have not expired.

The Stoics · Daoist Texts · Buddhist Suttas · Jewish Wisdom · Mystic Voices · Ancient Fragments

Poetry

Poetry does what philosophy cannot: it bypasses the arguing mind and lands in the body. These poems enact Arbora's values rather than merely describing them.

Basho · Mary Oliver · Wendell Berry · Gary Snyder · Robinson Jeffers · Yehuda Amichai · Kabir

Prayers & Invocations

Adapted from existing traditions — Jewish brachot, Buddhist chants, indigenous prayers — stripped of theistic framing. The form is a gift; the words are yours to shape.

Morning · Meals · Seasonal · Community Gathering · Ancestor Remembrance · Wilderness

Ancestral & Historical Texts

Exile, survival, cultural persistence, and the long memory of peoples who endured. Ancestry is not abstract — it is particular: specific names, towns, crossings.

Simon Dubnow · Primo Levi · Yehuda Amichai · S. Ansky · Frederick Douglass · Czeslaw Milosz

Scientific & Natural History

Science, practiced with attention and humility, is a form of revelation. Empirical fact rendered as sacred story — scripture for those who find the universe more astonishing the more precisely they look.

Suzanne Simard · Peter Wohlleben · John McPhee · Stefano Mancuso · Charles Darwin · Rachel Carson

Contemporary Companions

Modern works that reach toward the same truths — in a contemporary register. They are the canopy, not the roots. They belong here because they are unusually good.

Kimmerer · Taleb · Harris · Pirsig · McPhee · Muir · Diamond · Davis · Mitchell · Postman
