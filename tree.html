The Tree

The full structure of Arbora — a data structure & a symbol

Arbora is organized as a tree—both because trees are what we revere, and because a tree structure allows engagement at any depth. You can stay at the root (core values), explore a branch (cosmology, principles, practices), or follow a twig to its leaf (a specific prayer, a specific story, a specific practice for a Tuesday morning in winter).

The tree grows. New branches appear. Old ones are pruned or grafted. The structure is alive.
